# Wireframe Template — Small Business Website

Use this as the starting point for any new client wireframe. Copy to `clients/<name>/design/wireframe.md` and customize.

---

## Page: Home

### Section 1 — Hero
- **Layout:** Full-width, single background image or gradient
- **Content:** Headline (8 words max) · Subhead (1 sentence) · Primary CTA button
- **Notes:** Phone number visible top-right on desktop; sticky on mobile

### Section 2 — Services Overview
- **Layout:** 3-column card grid (stacks on mobile)
- **Content:** Icon + title + 1-sentence description per service (3–6 cards)
- **CTA:** "View All Services" link

### Section 3 — About / Trust
- **Layout:** 2-column (image left, text right)
- **Content:** Photo of owner/team · 2–3 sentence "why us" blurb · Years in business, license #, etc.
- **CTA:** "Meet the Team" or "About Us" link

### Section 4 — Testimonials / Social Proof
- **Layout:** Carousel or 3-column cards
- **Content:** 2–4 customer quotes with name and city · Star rating if available
- **Source:** Google Reviews, Yelp, or provided by client

### Section 5 — Call to Action
- **Layout:** Centered band, contrasting background
- **Content:** Headline ("Ready to get started?") · Phone number · CTA button ("Get a Free Quote")

### Section 6 — Footer
- **Layout:** 3-column (contact info | quick links | service area map or list)
- **Content:** Address · Phone · Email · Hours · Social icons · Phoenix-area service cities

---

## Page: Services (if multi-page)
- Individual service detail with: heading, description, photo, pricing hint, CTA

## Page: Contact
- Form: Name, Phone, Email, Message, preferred contact method
- Embedded Google Map
- Business hours and phone number repeated

---

## Design Notes
- Mobile-first — all sections must work single-column
- Target load time: < 2s on 4G
- Accessibility: AA contrast, readable font sizes, alt text on all images
