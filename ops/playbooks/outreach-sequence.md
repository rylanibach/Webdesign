# Playbook: Outreach Sequence

**Owner:** Emily | **Triggered by:** Kyle after Gate 1 approval

## Sequence timeline
```
Day 0  — First touch (owner approves before send)
Day +4 — Follow-up 1 (owner approves before send)
Day +7 — Follow-up 2 (owner approves before send)
Day +7 — If no reply: mark lead "Stale", stop sequence
```

---

## Email templates

### First touch
**Subject:** Quick question about [Business Name]'s website

Hi [First name if known, otherwise "there"],

I was searching for [industry] in [city] and came across [Business Name]. [One specific observation about their site — e.g., "I noticed your site doesn't show up well on phones" or "I couldn't find a way to request a quote online."]

We build websites for local [industry] businesses in the Phoenix area — fast, mobile-friendly, and built to bring in phone calls. We did a quick audit of your site and found a few things we could fix.

Would you be open to a 10-minute call this week? I can show you what we found and what it'd cost to fix it. No pressure.

[Signature]

---

### Follow-up 1 (Day +4)
**Subject:** Re: Quick question about [Business Name]'s website

Hi [name],

Just circling back on my note from earlier this week. I know things get busy.

We're currently taking on a couple of new clients in [city] — just wanted to see if the timing might work for [Business Name].

Even if it's not the right time, I'm happy to share what we found in our audit — no strings attached.

[Signature]

---

### Follow-up 2 (Day +7)
**Subject:** Last note — [Business Name]

Hi [name],

I'll keep this short — I don't want to clutter your inbox.

If you ever want to talk about your website, feel free to reply here or grab a time: [calendar link if available].

If the timing's off, totally understood. Best of luck with [Business Name].

[Signature]

---

## Research checklist (before drafting first touch)
- [ ] Business name confirmed
- [ ] Owner/contact name found (check site About page, GBP, LinkedIn)
- [ ] Contact email found (check site footer, contact page, GBP)
- [ ] Specific site issue documented (screenshot or URL noted)
- [ ] City and industry confirmed correct

## File naming
Save each draft as: `clients/_template/comms/outreach-draft-<business-slug>.md`
Example: `outreach-draft-phoenix-hvac-pros.md`
