Our Services

Full-service landscaping built for the Sonoran desert.

🌵 Xeriscaping

Low-water desert designs with native plants, decorative rock, and drip irrigation. We'll cut your water bill in half while making your yard the best-looking one on the block.

  • • Native plant selection & installation
  • • Decorative rock & gravel beds
  • • Drip irrigation integration
  • • Desert-adapted design plans

🌿 Lawn Care

Weekly mowing, fertilization, overseeding, and seasonal cleanups. We keep Phoenix yards green even when the thermometer says they shouldn't be.

  • • Weekly / biweekly mowing
  • • Seasonal fertilization
  • • Winter overseed (ryegrass)
  • • Spring & fall cleanups

🌳 Tree Trimming

Safe, certified pruning for palm, mesquite, ironwood, and citrus. We keep your trees healthy and your property looking sharp.

  • • Palm tree skinning & trimming
  • • Mesquite & palo verde shaping
  • • Citrus tree care
  • • Storm damage cleanup

💧 Irrigation

Smart drip systems, repairs, and timer upgrades that save water year-round. We design irrigation around your specific plant zones.

  • • Drip system design & install
  • • Timer/controller upgrades
  • • Leak detection & repair
  • • Seasonal adjustment schedules

🧱 Paver Installation

Patios, walkways, and driveways built to last decades under Arizona sun. We handle design, prep, and installation.

  • • Patio design & construction
  • • Walkways & garden paths
  • • Driveway extensions
  • • Retaining walls
