# Services Page Copy Template

Use this as a starting point for any client's services page. Customize per industry and client.

---

## Hero Section

**Headline pattern:** "[What You Do] for [Location] [Customers]"
- Example: "Expert Landscaping for Phoenix Homeowners"
- Example: "Mobile Auto Repair Across the East Valley"

**Subhead pattern:** "[Benefit] + [Trust signal]"
- Example: "From weekly maintenance to full yard transformations — licensed, bonded, and insured."

---

## Services Grid

Write 3-6 service cards. Each follows this structure:

### [Service Name]
**Title:** 3-5 words (e.g., "Weekly Lawn Maintenance")
**Description:** 1-2 sentences. Lead with the customer benefit, then describe what's included.

**Template:**
> **[Service Name]**
> [Customer benefit in one sentence]. [What's included — be specific]. [Price hint or "call for quote" if applicable].

**Examples by industry:**

### Landscaping
- **Weekly Lawn Maintenance** — Keep your yard looking sharp all year, even through the Phoenix summer. Mowing, edging, blowing, and trimming included.
- **Desert Landscaping** — Low-water, low-maintenance designs that thrive in Arizona heat. We handle design, install, and cleanup.
- **Tree Trimming & Removal** — Safe, clean removal for overgrown palms, mesquite, and palo verde. Same-week scheduling available.

### Auto Repair (Mobile)
- **On-Site Diagnostics** — We come to your driveway with full scan tools. Know what's wrong before you commit to a repair.
- **Brake Service** — Pads, rotors, and fluid replaced at your location. No tow truck, no waiting room.
- **AC Recharge** — Phoenix summers are no joke. We'll get your AC blowing cold again, on the spot.

### Paving / Concrete
- **Driveway Installation** — Pavers, stamped concrete, or traditional pour — we do it all. Free on-site estimate included.
- **Patio & Pool Deck** — Cool-touch pavers designed for Arizona sun. Slip-resistant, heat-reflective options available.
- **Repairs & Resurfacing** — Cracks, settling, or fading? We'll make it look new without full replacement.
